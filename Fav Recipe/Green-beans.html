Green Beans

Here is a nice quick side dish to go with your salmon.
While your waiting on your salmon to cook you can have this
done in half the time and it will certainly add to the dish.

Ingredients

  • Pack of green beans
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 2 tbsp Butter

Instructions

  1. Add your green beans to a pan.
  2. Add some boiling water and leave boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Next you want to drain the water and put the green beans back in the pan.
  4. Then you want to add the butter and garlic and let simmer for 2-3 mins.
  5. Your green beans are now ready to pop on a plate and enjoy.
