Potatoes

This is a must make to go with your salmon or any dish when you're including potatoes,
they are delicious and again so simple to make that you just have to include them. I tend
to go for the red potatoes because they seem to have a better crunch when they're done
but the choice is yours.

Ingredients

  • Red Potatoes
  • Garlic Granules
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2-3 tbsp Butter
  1. Pre-heat the oven at 220 dgrees C
  2. First you want to start cutting your potatoes into cube, skin on/off is your prefrence.
  3. Put them into a pot amd cover with boiling water.
  4. When water starts to boil set a timer for 8 minutes.
  5. When timer is up drain the water and put the potatoes back in the empty pot.
  6. Next melt your butter in a seperate pot.
  7. Add some salt and pepper to the potatoes.
  8. Now your going to sprinkle the garlic granules over the potatoes.
  9. When the butter has melted pour all over the potatoes.
  10. Line a baking tray with some parchment paper and toss the potatoes in.
  11. Leave in the oven for 15 mins, take them out give them a shake then pop them back in
    for another 15 mins until crispy.
