Bellow I have put a list of my favorite CD's and a brief desciption about them.

Feel No Shame
Aslan are a five piece rock band from Dublin that took Ireland by storm in the mid-eighties. This is their debut album.
Shame About Lucy Moonhead
Shame about Lucy Moonhead is a best of CD of some of Aslans classic songs and if you havent listened to Aslan yet you should start with this CD.
For Some Strange Reason
It doesn't live up to earlier releases but is still a worthwhile additional to the CD collection.
